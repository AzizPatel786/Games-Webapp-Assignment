About Kelvin Games

Welcome to Kelvin Games – a platform that redefines the gaming experience. At Kelvin Games, our mission is to provide you with an unparalleled journey into the world of gaming, where boundaries are shattered, and possibilities are endless.

Our platform showcases a meticulously curated collection of games that cater to diverse tastes, from heart-pounding action to brain-teasing puzzles, epic RPGs to innovative indies. Each game has been handpicked to meet the highest standards of quality, ensuring an exceptional experience for every gamer.

What sets Kelvin Games apart is not just the games we offer, but how we offer them. Our user-friendly interface and advanced recommendation system tailor your gaming journey to your preferences, ensuring you discover new titles that resonate with your taste.

Kelvin Games is powered by the latest web technologies, including the Flask framework. Our dedicated team of developers, including Mihir Patil, Aziz Patel, and Senu Senathirajah, hailing from the University of Auckland, has crafted this platform to seamlessly connect gamers with the content they love.

Thank you for joining us on this exciting adventure. Experience the future of gaming with Kelvin Games – where entertainment knows no bounds.

About the Developers

Meet the minds behind Kelvin Games - Mihir Patil, Aziz Patel, and Senu Senathirajah. We're passionate second-year Computer Science students at the University of Auckland, aged 18, 19, and 20. Our diverse skills and shared love for technology led us to embark on this journey to create an innovative gaming platform that caters to players of all types.

Mihir is an avid cricket player and gym enthusiast. Aziz is a gaming aficionado who enjoys titles like Clash of Clans and Call of Duty, alongside his passion for fitness. Senu is a driven student pursuing a conjoint degree in Computer Science and Finance, he is a gun soccer player as well. Together, we're united by our goal to deliver a top-tier gaming experience through Kelvin Games.
